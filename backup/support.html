What is Triangle?

Triangle is a Dreamweaver Extension that lets you design on our platform, but in the environment you're used to. Because it's a Dreamweaver Extension, it integrates directly with your Dreamweaver workspace (on both Mac and PC). As long as you've got an internet connection, you'll be able to build your Online Business and customize it's features, all from Dreamweaver.

System Requirements

  • Mac: Dreamweaver CS3 or CS4
  • Windows: Dreamweaver 8, CS3 or CS4
